How Display Order Works

  • Display Order 0 — Vehicle appears in the Best Sellers carousel on the home page, as well as its category carousel.
  • Display Order 1+ — Vehicle appears only in its category carousel, sorted lowest-number-first.
  • Unpublished — Vehicle is saved as a draft and hidden from the public site entirely.
